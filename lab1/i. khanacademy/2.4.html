Its has beatiful mountains

Kazakhstan is mostly a flat country, the bulk of the territory is occupied by semi-deserts, steppes and forest-steppes. Large mountains are located in the border areas, in the south and east of the country.
Only the Altai Mountains are located a considerable distance from Almaty; the rest of the mountainous regions can be reached quite quickly by car. It takes 30 minutes to drive to the closest one - Trans-Ili Alatau. The farthest ones - the mountains of the Central Tien Shan and the Dzhungar Alatau - can be reached in 4-5 hours by highway.

There a are lot of different animals at our lands


The diversity of a flora of Kazakhstan varies considerably both by structure and number of taxa, and by geography, in particular, by nature-climatic zones and high-altitude belts. In Kazakhstan there are more than 6,000 species of highest vascular plants, about 5,000 species of mushrooms, 485 species of lichen, and more than 2,010 species of seaweed.
